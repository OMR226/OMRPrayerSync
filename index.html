Smart prayer reminders for Windows.

What it does

⏱

Accurate prayer times

Prayer times stay in sync based on your location and chosen calculation method (with Hanafi/Standard Asr support).

🕌

Adhan at official time

Plays Adhan audio (per prayer toggle). Your notifications and voice reminders will never talk over the Adhan.

🔔

Reminder after a delay

Get a reminder after 30 minutes (or your custom minutes per prayer) that says it’s time to pray and how long it’s been since Adhan.

🧠

Smart next-prayer countdown

Notifications can include how long until the next prayer, so you can plan your time.

📖

Surah Al-Kahf weekly reminder

Friday reminder (default 2:00 PM). Optional MP3 playback — automatically delayed if Adhan is currently playing.

🔒

Privacy-friendly

Your location is used only to calculate prayer times. The app runs locally (tray + service).

How it works

Notifier (Tray App) runs under your logged-in Windows user and shows notifications, plays audio, and provides Settings UI.

Service runs in the background and calculates schedules + triggers events (Adhan + reminders) using secure local IPC.

Designed so the reminders keep working even if location becomes temporarily stale.

FAQ

Will voice reminders speak over Adhan?

No. The app queues TTS and only speaks after Adhan finishes playing.

Can I disable Surah Al-Kahf reminders or audio?

Yes. You can disable reminder, disable audio, and customize the Friday time.

Does it require internet?

Prayer times are fetched from an API. Audio can be auto-downloaded once (and then cached locally).

Where is the installer hosted?

On the public GitHub repo’s Releases page. Your source code can stay private in a separate repo.
